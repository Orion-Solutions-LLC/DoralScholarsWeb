Youth Development Program

A safe, supportive, and enriching environment fostering positive development, conflict resolution skills, and community engagement.

Program Goal

To provide a safe, supportive, and enriching environment for youth, fostering positive development, conflict resolution skills, and community engagement to prevent violence and crime.

Program Structure

Each month focuses on a specific theme, with weekly activities building upon that theme. Activities include workshops, discussions, creative projects, physical activities, and community service opportunities.

Monthly Program Calendar

January: "Fresh Start, Safe Start" & "Resilience in Unity"

Kick off the new year by focusing on goal setting, community resilience, and creating a safe environment. Participants will learn conflict resolution techniques, create personal and community vision boards, and engage in team-building activities to strengthen community bonds. We will explore what resilience means, and hear stories from community members who have overcome adversity.

Weekly Activities:

  • Goal-setting workshops
  • Conflict resolution skill-building
  • Community vision board creation
  • Resilience storytelling and discussions

February: "Love Our Community" & "Healthy Relationships Matter"

This month emphasizes the importance of healthy relationships and community care. Participants will learn about healthy communication, respect, and boundaries, and engage in community service projects to show their love for their neighborhood. We will also focus on recognizing and addressing unhealthy relationship dynamics.

Weekly Activities:

  • Healthy relationship workshops
  • Community service projects (e.g., park cleanups, assisting seniors)
  • Role-playing scenarios for conflict resolution
  • Discussions on positive communication

March - December

Additional monthly themes and activities continue throughout the year. Contact us for the complete program calendar.
